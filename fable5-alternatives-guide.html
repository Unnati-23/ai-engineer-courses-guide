Fable 5 is gone.
Here's what to use instead.

On June 12, 2026, the US government issued an export control directive forcing Anthropic to disable Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 worldwide — just 4 days after launch. Your work doesn't stop. Here's your cheat sheet.

What actually happened

The US government, citing national security concerns, ordered Anthropic to block any foreign national, anywhere — including Anthropic's own non-US employees — from using Fable 5 and Mythos 5. To comply, Anthropic had to disable both models for everyone, everywhere, overnight.

The government believes someone found a "jailbreak" — a way to bypass the model's safety rules. Anthropic disagrees with the decision and says it's working to restore access. All other Claude models remain unaffected.
