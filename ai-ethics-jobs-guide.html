Study Roadmap

WHAT YOU NEED TO LEARN

You don't need to code. But you need to understand AI well enough to govern it. Here's what to focus on:

  • 1
    AI Fundamentals (non-technical) How AI works, types of models, what LLMs are, bias & fairness basics. No maths needed. → AI For Everyone by Andrew Ng (Free on Coursera)
  • 2
    AI Ethics & Fairness Frameworks Learn NIST AI Risk Management Framework, EU AI Act basics, and OECD AI Principles. → NIST AI RMF (Free)
  • 3
    EU AI Act (August 2026 enforcement) The single biggest driver of hiring right now. Understand high-risk AI categories, obligations, and penalties. → EU AI Act Explorer (Free)
  • 4
    Risk Management & Compliance Basics GRC (Governance, Risk, Compliance) concepts. ISO 42001 AI Management Standard. → ISO 42001 Overview
  • 5
    Build a Portfolio (no code needed) Write an AI Policy doc for a real/fake company. Conduct a bias audit report on a public AI tool. Document your findings in a PDF or Notion page. This is your proof of work.
